The Foundations

One Platform. Four Foundations.

OML Code fuses knowledge engineering, formal logic, AI, and DevOps into a single intelligence-centric engineering platform.

Knowledge Engineering

Models are not pictures; they are formal representations of engineering knowledge: concepts, relationships, constraints, and intent in machine-understandable form.

Formal Logic

Formal logic turns static knowledge into actionable knowledge through automated inference, classification, consistency checking, and validation.

AI

AI generates, explains, queries, and navigates engineering models while remaining grounded in formal semantics, so answers stay trustworthy.

DevOps

Engineering knowledge becomes a first-class artifact: version controlled, automatically validated, peer reviewed, and continuously delivered.
