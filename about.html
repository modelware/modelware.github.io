Who We Are

A Decade in the Making

Modelware is a US-based company with a single mission: to make engineering knowledge formal, computable, and trustworthy. We believe the models engineers build should be something you can reason on and compute over, not static documents that drift out of date.

That conviction did not appear overnight. It was forged over more than a decade of research, now carried forward by Modelware Labs, and proven in the open through the openCAESAR platform on some of the most demanding engineering programs in the world.

OML Code is the result: an intelligence-centric engineering platform that brings that rigor to any organization, built on three foundations, Logic, AI, and DevOps.

What We Stand On

Built on Three Foundations

Everything we build rests on the same three pillars.

Logic

Formal, machine-understandable models with the semantics to be reasoned over and verified, not just drawn.

AI

DevOps

Git-native versioning and continuous validation that operationalize engineering knowledge across the lifecycle.
