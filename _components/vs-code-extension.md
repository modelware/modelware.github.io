Engineers should not have to leave the environment they love to do serious modeling. The OML Code extension brings the full platform into VS Code, the editor millions of engineers already use every day, on the desktop or in the browser.

Authoring feels like writing code because it is. You get complete language intelligence for OML: syntax highlighting, real-time validation, content assist, and go-to-definition. The graphical notation stays in sync with the text, so you read diagrams and write text, or the other way around, whichever suits the moment.

The extension is also where the rest of the platform comes together. Methodology viewpoints guide your work and present the right information at each step. Markdown documents and mind maps link directly to model information. The reasoner flags issues as you type. And the built-in AI copilot answers questions, drafts model content, and explains unfamiliar parts of the model, all grounded in your formal knowledge.

#### What you get

- Full OML language intelligence: validation, content assist, navigation, refactoring
- Text and diagrams that are two views of the same model, never out of sync
- Methodology-driven viewpoints, documents, and dashboards in the same workspace
- An AI copilot grounded in your model, ready to query, generate, and explain
- The same experience on desktop and in the browser, with no installation barrier for reviewers

If your engineers already know VS Code, they already know half of OML Code. A demo covers the other half in under an hour.
