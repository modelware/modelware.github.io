Profile

Dr. Maged Elaasar is the Founder and CEO of Modelware. He is a computer scientist and software engineer with long-standing leadership across model-driven engineering and ontology-based systems work.

He previously led model-centric and ontological engineering initiatives in aerospace and defense programs and has contributed to international standards and research communities.

Education
  • Ph.D. in Computer Science, Carleton University, Canada
  • M.C.S. in Computer Science, Carleton University, Canada
  • B.Sc. in Computer Science, American University in Cairo, Egypt
