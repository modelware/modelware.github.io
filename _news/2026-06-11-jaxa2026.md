          knowledge-centric to intelligence-centric, and asked why so many MBSE efforts stall after the first models
          are built. The answer: creating models was never the goal. The goal is engineering knowledge that teams can
          trust and act on, and that AI can be safely grounded in.
        </p>
        <p class="text-left">
          The session also featured live demonstrations. Modelware partner David Wagner demonstrated a verification and
          validation methodology built on the platform, and Yuta Nakajima showed how JAXA has been leveraging the
          platform to streamline model-based analysis in their context.
        </p>
