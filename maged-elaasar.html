Profile

Dr. Maged Elaasar is the Founder and CEO of Modelware, the company behind OML Code, the intelligence-centric engineering platform.

With over 25 years spanning industry, government, and academia, he brings rare depth to systems engineering. He has spent 12+ years at NASA Jet Propulsion Laboratory (JPL) where he has been the main PI behind the Integrated Model Centric Engineering (IMCE) program, a strategic effort to make systems engineering more rigorous, agile, and data-driven across complex space programs. He is a lecturer at UCLA and Northeastern University, and previously spent 13 years at IBM leading R&D on flagship modeling tools, earning 12 US patents and the distinction of IBM Master Inventor.

He is the lead developer of openCAESAR, the open-source platform for ontological modeling and analysis proven on demanding engineering programs, and has shaped international standards at the OMG as UML co-chair and SysML contributor. He has authored over 85 peer-reviewed papers, serves as President of the INCOSE MENA Chapter, and chairs the onto:Nexus Forum.

Through Modelware Labs and openCAESAR, he has spent more than a decade turning foundational research and mission-grade engineering experience into OML Code, bringing that rigor to any organization engineering complex systems.
