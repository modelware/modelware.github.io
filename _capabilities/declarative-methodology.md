The biggest gap between MBSE theory and MBSE practice is methodology. Tools give engineers infinite freedom, then organizations wonder why every model looks different. OML Code closes that gap by making the methodology itself a first-class, declarative artifact.

A methodology in OML Code consists of vocabularies, patterns, visualizations, and analyses. Because it is declarative, it is adaptable to your organization, evolvable as your practice matures, and it actively guides engineers as they create and analyze models. Everyone follows the same method because the method is executable, not a PDF on a shelf.

#### Why it changes adoption

- New team members become productive in days, guided by the method instead of tribal knowledge
- Models stay methodologically consistent across teams, projects, and years
- Your method evolves by editing a model, not by retraining the whole organization
- Stakeholders engage through viewpoints tailored to their concerns

This is how you scale MBSE beyond the few experts who carry it today.
