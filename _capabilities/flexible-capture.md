No two people on an engineering team capture information the same way. Systems engineers think in models, domain experts think in tables, managers think in documents, and everyone is starting to think in prompts. OML Code meets all of them where they are.

Whether knowledge arrives through the textual editor, a form-based user interface, a file import, or a GenAI prompt, it lands in the same formal model with the same rigor. Nothing is a second-class citizen and nothing gets lost in translation.

#### Why teams care

- Modeling experts get a fast, code-like authoring experience with full language intelligence
- Non-modelers contribute through interfaces that feel like the tools they already know
- Existing data assets become model content instead of staying trapped in files
- AI-assisted capture lowers the entry barrier for everyone on the team

This is how MBSE adoption actually spreads inside an organization: not by forcing everyone to become a modeler, but by letting everyone feed the model.

Want to see your own spreadsheet become a verified model in minutes? That makes a great demo.
