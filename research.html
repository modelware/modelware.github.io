Built on a Decade of Research

OML Code did not start as a product idea. It grew out of years of research in ontological modeling and rigorous systems engineering, with roots at NASA's Jet Propulsion Laboratory and collaborations spanning corporate, government, and academic laboratories. That research foundation is why the platform can make claims most tools cannot.

Flagship Initiatives

Three long-running initiatives carry our research from idea to open practice.

Open Source Platform

openCAESAR

The open source platform for ontological modeling and analysis, created at NASA JPL and proven on flagship space missions. openCAESAR pioneered the rigorous, model-as-code approach that OML Code productizes today.

Visit openCAESAR
